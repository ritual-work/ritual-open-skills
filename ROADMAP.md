# Roadmap

Lightweight and honest — direction, not commitments.

## v1 — curated standalone skills (now)

- 10 strong, self-contained **engineering** skills in the publish set.
- 24 standalone skills rendered across Engineering / Product / Marketing for
  inspection; 45 discovery drafts as raw material.
- Standalone-first, token-free, MIT-0; optional Ritual Cloud upgrade.
- Publish stays gated until each allowlisted skill passes the
  [quality rubric](./docs/skill-quality-rubric.md).

## v2 — more skills, by quality

- Author standalone bodies for the strongest **product / GTM / support** candidates
  and promote them into the publish set after review.
- Grow the catalog by usefulness and real install/usage signal — not by hitting a
  target count.

## v3 — deeper upgrade paths (if demand)

- Self-hosted / local Ritual upgrade path (v1 is Cloud-only).
- Discovery-mode skills with full authored standalone bodies (today they're drafts).

## Always

- Generated, never hand-edited. Single source of truth in the Ritual catalog.
- Small and excellent over large and thin.
